GlowStep Simulator

Initialization:

First add the required number of glowsteps on the playground. This is done by selecting the amount in the input field and arranging them either in a grid or randomly.

After this you can drag them around by clicking and dragging. Once you're satisfied with the layout, press the start simulation button.

Simulation:

Click a glowstep to mimic someone stepping on it. You can hold shift and drag it around. The nearest neighbours will be recalculated on dropping the glowstep.
